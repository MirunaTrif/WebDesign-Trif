UniO is about us

UniO is an NFT collection of 240 evolving Utility, art-focused assets on the MultiversX Blockchain.
 
Rather than focusing on the physical side of humanoid beings, our vision takes the path of describing humans' metaphysical reality. Moreover, it focuses on our dual nature, a child's play between light and darkness - both sincere and mysterious, both joyful and meditative.

This collection is an act of meditation and introspection itself. 



Features

Passive Income

A cross-chain 5% of all future Fine Art sales;
+ 10 % of UniO royalties

Community Value

 Holders can improve UniO's value by adding, proposing & voting additional utilities. There is no limitation on the total number of available benefits.

The overall utility becomes community-built.

The Artwork

Every element used for the creation of this collection is a digitized piece of a hand-painted professional watercolour paper (300 gsm).

There are 8 components & complete types.
